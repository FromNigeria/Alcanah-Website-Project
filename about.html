FEW WORDS FROM US

Alcanah Limited is an indigenous manufacturing company established in 2000 for manufacturing of African Black Soap and other unique beauty products from natural ingredients

We strive to bring you top-tier completely natural products that you can not only connect with, but also adopt into your household, family, and life. Literally, We're just like those chips baby, once you try one, you just can't stop. All our products now or in the future is to make sure that you have all you need in one place. We will be dropping more products in the near future so definitely be on the lookout.

Since opening, our African Black soap brand has been the darling washing and bathing products for numerous houselholds across the world, Africa and Nigeria by extension. Alcanah natural beauty product is recognized for its commitment to 100% natural products. We are here to instill and bring back that confidence in your beauty and skin. Helping women, men, youths, kids everywhere develop a good relationship with their skin - to love their skin, also from the way they see themselves from a beauty sense by helping to uplift their self-esteem realising their inner beauty potential with natural products that deliver with care taking inspiration from nature encouraging us to bring natural beauty to homes across the world.

At Alcanah Limited, we produced top quality cosmetic products from the best of natural ingredients leveraging on the power of nature to restore your natural beauty. Currently, we have 4 product variants namely: African Zee Black Soap, Needz with Shea Butter soap, Needz with Aloe Vera and Shea Butter soap & Needz with Native Honey and Shea Butter soap. To us, It's always reassuring how our products have helped transform daily people's life, having so much wonderful stories to share to others about their skin transformation. Feeling good about your skin and loving it is what drives our motivation.
Our tremendous success story has been through the wave of positive feedbacks from all our customers and hence, we are highly encourage to do more for you..

We are dedicated to helping you discover the joy of happy and healthy skin just the way you like it!
