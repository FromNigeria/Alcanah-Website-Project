Always allow your soap dry out between uses, by putting your soap on some kind of a soap dish that allows air to circulate around the bar. Normally, a 'proper' soap dish will either raise the bar up for air to circulate from under, or contain air holes for drainage of water below the soap. If you store your soap in the little alcove built into most shower stalls and tub surrounds, the shower spray will continually soak your soap. Our natural soaps will last longer if you take care of them!
