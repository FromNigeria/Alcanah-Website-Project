Major IngredientsWe believe in keeping our ingredients simple and natural

Shea Butter

Is an extraction of the Shea nuts, which is a product of the Karite tree which grows wild in abundance on the west coast of African countries like Ghana, Togo. Rich in Stearic, Oleic acids, linolenic, palmitic acid Vitamin E & A.

Benefits

Highly moisturizing | Helps fades dark mark and spots | Nourishes the skin to prevent drying | Great to use on Kids and Babies too | Wade of stretch marks during pregnancy | Combats dry skin, skin irritations | Wrinkle Reduction | Fights Eczema

Aloe Vera

As natural ingredients is a non-toxic plant of family Xanthorrhoeaceae Commonly known as plant of 'immortality for its healing properties' and has over 200 active components and contains vitamins including A, C, E, Folic acid, minerals, enzymes, amino-acids, choline, B1, B2, B3(niacin), B6

Benefits

Treats Acne and Acne scars. | Exfoliates the Skin for revealing a glowing skin. | Great for anyone with oily skin complexion | Cleanser | For women who use mineral-based make-up, aloe vera acts as a Moisturizer and is great for the face prior to application to prevent skin drying | Makeup Removal

Native Honey

Is naturally antibacterial in nature and full of antioxidants. Antioxidants are really good for us which means substances like Vitamin C or E present in raw Honey helps removes potentially damaging agents in skincare products or even foods.

Benefits

Combats Acne | Complexion boost: extremely moisturizing and soothing | Raw honey for a Skin Cleanser and clearer skin to prevent skin breakouts | Treat Skin Dryness | Fights Dark Spots and Scar | Skin blemishes or Irritations.
